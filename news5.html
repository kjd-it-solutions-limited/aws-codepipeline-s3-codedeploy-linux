Free Training!
Does it work?.
 

With so many free training sites available

offering such a large number of "Technical" certifications when training is completed.

We ask , Do they really work,

Are they worth the effort?

Are they better than the subscription training?

 

Throughout the years We have attended many training courses, some inhouse with clients and some residential.

We have also completed many online or eLearning courses.

What do we prefer, What has moved us forward in the search for the right role.

 

From our past experience, Residential courses seem to get the information in easier and quicker.

but these are always costly, and can also prove to be of little use, when proving efficiency of a subject.

 

The free options some sites such as Alison.com can provide a wide range of free courses,

often with a 'Diploma' or other certification (extra fees may apply).

These courses can be quite easy to complete. some may have examinations at the end, and most are video based.training.

however the huge range of courses available from sites like these can leave you undecided as to where you want to aim for.

Our advise is to review existing goals, plan the courses you want to do. and then start of with the free options.

once you have completed the free courses, then if you wish to get deeper knowledge, opt for the residential courses.
