Services!
KJD Solutions have worked within a wide variety of IT spheres.
 

We are capable of working on small to medium projects,

as well as providing support for a variety of applications, running on any operating system,

Specialising in UNIX/Linux Operating systems.


We can also provide support for the full Microsoft range and can support DHCP and Active Directory.

Servers builds and infrastructure within physical locations are another speciality;

as well as utilising Virtualization techniques including VMware, Hyper V and of course AWS.


We can produce scripts to aid complex tasks, as well as create automated build scripts,

using kickstart or Bootstraps scripts.


If specific scripting requirements are being considered, let us know, as ad-hoc linux scripts

are a great option for those more complex configurations and we can give you that flexibility


We can provide a full design and implementation service, and are capable of working

with your own teams of if preferred, utilizing our own resources to provide the full picture.


If you need remote support or consultance, then let us know, as we are more than capable of

supporting you from our office.


We are also able to provide you with virtual servers, using our own Virtual Hosts or securely

through our own AWS Infrastructure.

This also allows us to set up development and testing environments to allow safe and secure upgrades

without affecting produciton environments.


Our high speed network allows us to be just as efficient remotely as we would be on-site.
